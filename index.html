About Me

My name is Eric Guerrero. I have a diverse work history. Two of my longest tenured postitions were: U.S. Navy 6 years and teaching kindergarten for 33 years. All of my experiences have led me to where I am today and are reflective of a lifelong pursuit of knowledge and growth. A quest I am still on today!


I have been completely enamored with technology for as long as I can remember. I hope to take my love of technology on a path to a new career as a Full Stack Web Developer. It is my hope that I will be able to contribute to the everchanging landscape of technology in a meaningful and helpful way.
